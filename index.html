Summary: Semi-Global Weather Analysis

The WeatherPy Project analyzed the weather in over 500 cities across different latitudes on the same day. Basically, we took a snapshot of the day's weather across the globe by pulling data from the OpenWeatherMap API. Then we cleaned up the numbers and crunched them using pandas. We used the dataset to plot different aspects of the weather across cities' latitudes. We specifically looked at how temperature, cloudiness, wind speed, and humidity differed across the globe on that particular day. Using our data, we built a site that allowed the world to visualize the patterns nicely.
